About Us

MR Tour Guide is a cultural adaptive mapping platform using mixed reality. Our mission is to:

  • Create a cultural map of heritage sites in Batangas.
  • Develop Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality applications for potential tourists.
  • Provide web and mobile platforms to access these VR and AR applications.
  • Conduct testing and validation for tourists to rate their experience and satisfaction.

This collaborative project is expected to develop a Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality of various heritage sites that can enhance the experience of the tourists regardless of their place. The application and software can be a great investment to develop as it could invite income in the tourism sector.
